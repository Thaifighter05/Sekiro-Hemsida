Welcome to The sekiro fanpage.

Sekiro:Shadows die twice is a Fromsoftware game with the genre of action-adventure.It's directed by Hidetaki Miyazaki and got realeased on March 22 in the year 2019, avaliable on most platforms like ps4, Xbox and microsoft windows.

Sekiro is inspired by ninja games but Sekiro Wolf himself is not a ninja. Set in the closing years of Sengoku era we follow the Shinobi through his journey to take down the Ashina clan and their leader Genchiro.
